I am an interdisciplinary researcher working at the intersection of machine learning, linguistics, and cognitive neuroscience. My PhD research studies how artificial and biological systems represent and anticipate linguistic structure, and how these representations help explain the relationship between large language models and human brain activity. I use language models, brain data, and interpretability methods such as probing and concept erasure.

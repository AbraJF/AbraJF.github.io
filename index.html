Abraham Jacob Fresen (Bram)

PhD Researcher · Language and Predictive Computation Group, Max Planck Institute for Psycholinguistics, Nijmegen

Part-time AI & Innovation Consultant · ClickValue

I study interpretable features like syntax

More precisely, my PhD research asks how artificial and biological systems represent and anticipate linguistic structure, and how human-interpretable features help explain the relationship between large language models and human brain activity. I work with language models, brain data, and interpretability methods such as probing and concept erasure.

Alongside my research, I work part-time as an AI & Innovation Consultant at ClickValue, building applied machine-learning systems and automation — from retrieval-augmented AI agents to causal-impact analytics pipelines.

Abraham Jacob Fresen (Bram)

PhD Researcher · Language and Predictive Computation Group, MPI for Psycholinguistics, Nijmegen

Part-time AI & Innovation Consultant · ClickValue

I am an interdisciplinary researcher working at the intersection of machine learning, linguistics, and cognitive neuroscience. My PhD studies predictive computation in language — how artificial and biological systems represent and anticipate linguistic structure — using language models, brain data, and interpretability methods such as probing and concept erasure.

Alongside research I work part-time as an AI & Innovation consultant and engineer at ClickValue, building applied machine-learning systems — from retrieval-augmented AI agents to causal-impact analytics pipelines.

Research interests: NeuroAI · predictive computation · model–brain alignment · interpretability & probing · computational linguistics
